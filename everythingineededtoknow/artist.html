About the Artist
Martine Syms is an LA based artistconceptual entrepreneur. Her work has been featured worlwide, including in the MoMA in New York, ICA in London, SFU Galleries in Vancouver, etc. Although her work usually revolves around feminism and black culture, she also explores identity and the portrayal of self, EverythingIneededtoknow.com being a great example of this.
