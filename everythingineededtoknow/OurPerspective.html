Ama's Thoughts' I found it interesting her focus was more on the act of searching itself rather than the terms searched. My dad always would say you kids are fortune to have the internet and Google when it comes time for you to find information. When he was kid you had to scour the library for books to find the information he needed. I did do that when I was in elementary school, but then here comes middle school, high school and college where everything is accessible. Yes, it is quite a benefit, but it actually uncomforatble to know someone I do not is collecting data on me based on my searches, likes, past purchases, etc. It reminds me I have to be mindful and honestly make me want to limit my social presence, but how can I do that when everything is made simply from the internet.
